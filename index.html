No dollars to spend, just hundreds of pennies.

That's been my unoffical "motto" of sorts for several years now. It's a short excerpt from an unreleased poem I wrote back in 2014, and it essentially points out how I feel about my own interests and abilities. I'm fascinated by countless activities and art forms, and I've dipped my toes into nearly every one that I can. However, I'm not great in any particular field. I'm just okay. This website is a portal to many mediocre fruits of my passions.

I make crappy songs, and I love doing it.
